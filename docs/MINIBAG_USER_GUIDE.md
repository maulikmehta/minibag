# Minibag User Guide

**Simple guide for buying vegetables with your neighbors**

---

## What is Minibag?

Minibag helps you coordinate vegetable shopping with your neighbors. Instead of buying alone, you can plan together and get better prices when the vegetable vendor comes to your area.

**Think of it like this:** When you and your neighbors buy together, the vendor is happy to come to your location because they can sell more at once. You get better prices, and everyone saves time.

**Example:**
You want to buy 1kg tomatoes. Your 4 neighbors also want tomatoes. When the vendor sees 5kg total demand, they're more likely to come to your building at your preferred time, and might give you a better rate too.

---

## Why Use Minibag?

### For You:
- **Better prices** - Vendors give discounts when selling more
- **Save time** - No more waiting around for the vendor
- **Fresh vegetables** - Vendors bring fresh stock when they know demand
- **Know what to expect** - See total cost before shopping

### For Your Neighbors:
- **Easy to join** - Just click a link, no app to download
- **Privacy protected** - Your phone number is never shared
- **Flexible** - Add or remove items anytime before shopping

---

## Two Ways to Use Minibag

### Option A: Start a List (Host)
You create the shopping list and invite your neighbors to add their items.

### Option B: Join a List (Participant)
Someone else already started a list, and you're joining to add your vegetables.

---

## How to Start a List (For Hosts)

### Step 1: Create Your List

1. Open Minibag on your phone
2. Click **"New list"**
3. Enter your name (just first name is fine)
4. Add your vegetables and quantities
   - Example: Tomatoes - 2kg, Onions - 1kg

**Tip:** You don't need to create an account or sign in!

---

### Step 2: Share with Neighbors

1. Click **"Share link"** after creating your list
2. Send the link via WhatsApp to your building group or neighbors
3. Your neighbors can click the link and add their items

**What your neighbors will see:**
They'll see your name (like "Maulik") and what you're buying. They can then add their own items.

**Privacy note:** Your phone number is never shared. Only your first name appears.

---

### Step 3: Wait for Others to Join (Optional)

After sharing the link:
- You'll see who joined and what they're buying
- Everyone gets a cute 3-letter nickname (like "Dev" or "Mia")
- Total quantities update automatically as people add items

**If you're shopping alone:** That's okay too! You can use Minibag just for yourself to keep track of what you bought.

---

### Step 4: Go Shopping

When it's time to shop:
1. Go to the vendor at the scheduled time
2. Everyone buys their items normally
3. Vendor gives you items and tells you the price

**Minibag doesn't:**
- Take payment through the app
- Require advance payment
- Change how you normally buy

**Minibag does:**
- Help everyone know what to buy
- Calculate how much you owe
- Make it easy to share costs if buying together

---

### Step 5: Record Payments

After buying each item:
1. Open your Minibag list
2. Tap **"Add payment"** next to each item
3. Enter the amount you paid
4. Choose payment method (Cash or UPI)

**If an item wasn't good quality:**
- Tap the **"Skip"** checkbox
- This means you didn't buy it (quality wasn't good)
- It won't be included in your total

---

### Step 6: Share Bill Summary

After recording all payments:
1. Click **"Done shopping"**
2. Click **"Share bill"**
3. Send via WhatsApp to your neighbors

**What they get:**
A clear breakdown of who bought what and how much each person needs to pay.

**Example message:**
```
Shopping Summary - Nov 2, 2025

Maulik bought:
- Tomatoes 2kg - ₹80
- Onions 1kg - ₹40
Total: ₹120

Neha bought:
- Tomatoes 1kg - ₹40
Total: ₹40

[See full details: minibag.in/abc123]
```

## How to Join a List (For Participants)

### Step 1: Open the Link

1. Someone sends you a Minibag link via WhatsApp
2. Click the link to open it on your phone
3. You'll see what the host is buying

**No app needed!** It works right in your phone browser.

---

### Step 2: Add Your Name and Items

1. Enter your first name
2. Browse vegetable categories or search
3. Add items you want to buy
   - Example: Potatoes - 3kg, Tomatoes - 1kg

**Tip:** You can see what others are buying too!

---

### Step 3: Confirm Your List

1. Review your items
2. Click **"Confirm list"** when ready
3. That's it! You're done.

**Can I change items later?**
Yes! You can add or remove items anytime before shopping.

---

### Step 4: Go Shopping

1. Check the list for shopping time and location
2. Go to the location at that time
3. Buy your items from the vendor
4. Pay the vendor directly (cash or UPI)

**Simple!** Just buy like you normally would.

---

### Step 5: Check Your Total

After shopping:
1. The host will record payments
2. You'll get a WhatsApp message with your share
3. Check how much you spent

**If you paid the vendor directly:** You're done!
**If the host paid for everyone:** Send them money via UPI (if needed).

## Common Questions

### Do I need to create an account?
**No!** Just enter your first name when you join. No password, no email needed.

---

### Will my phone number be shared with others?
**No.** We never share your phone number. Others only see your first name and a fun 3-letter nickname like "Dev" or "Mia".

---

### What if the vendor doesn't have something I wanted?
The host can mark it as "Skipped" when recording payments. This means it wasn't bought (maybe quality wasn't good or vendor didn't have it). Skipped items don't count toward your total.

---

### Can I change my list after adding items?
**Yes!** Until shopping starts, you can:
- Add more items
- Remove items
- Change quantities

Just open the link again and make changes.

---

### Do I have to pay through the app?
**No.** Minibag doesn't take payments. You pay the vendor directly with cash or UPI, just like you normally do. Minibag just helps you keep track of what everyone bought.

---

### What if I can't make it to shopping?
Tell the host! They can either:
- Remove your items from the list
- Mark you as "not coming" so items aren't bought for you

---

### Can the vendor see our list?
Not yet, but we're working on that! For now, the host can show the vendor the total quantities if needed.

---

### Is this a shopping app like Blinkit or Zepto?
**No.** Minibag doesn't deliver groceries. It helps you coordinate with neighbors so your local vegetable vendor knows what you want and can bring the right quantities.

**Think of it as:** A smart shopping list that you share with neighbors.

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Hosts:

**1. Share the link early**
- Send the link 2-3 hours before shopping
- Gives neighbors time to add their items

**2. Set a clear time and place**
- "Tomorrow 6pm at Building A gate"
- "Today evening 5:30pm at society entrance"

**3. Confirm with neighbors**
- Send a reminder 30 minutes before shopping
- Check who's coming

**4. Mark items that weren't good**
- Use the "Skip" checkbox for poor quality items
- This keeps your total accurate

**5. Share the bill summary**
- After shopping, share the breakdown via WhatsApp
- Helps everyone see their share

---

### For Participants:

**1. Add items early**
- Don't wait until last minute
- Helps host know total quantities

**2. Confirm your list**
- Click "Confirm list" when you're sure
- Shows host you're coming

**3. Be on time**
- Arrive at the shopping time
- Don't keep others waiting

**4. Bring exact change (if paying cash)**
- Makes payment faster
- Vendor will appreciate it

---

## Privacy & Safety

### What we collect:
- Your first name (no last name needed)
- Items you want to buy
- Quantities

### What we DON'T collect:
- Phone numbers
- Email addresses
- Your exact address (only neighborhood level)
- Payment information (you pay vendor directly)

### How long we keep data:
- Active shopping lists: Until shopping is done
- After shopping: We delete personal details within 30 days
- We keep anonymous statistics (like "10 people bought tomatoes") to improve the app

### Who can see my information:
- Only people in your shopping session
- They see your first name and nickname
- They can't see your phone number or contact you directly
